Frequently Asked Questions

Your Questions, Answered Honestly

No sales spin — just clear answers to the questions we get asked every day.

Is MBA after B.Sc Nursing worth it in 2025?
Absolutely yes. With India adding 5,000+ hospitals and healthcare spending growing at 12% annually, demand for nurse-managers-turned-administrators has never been higher. Our alumni data shows B.Sc Nurses who completed this MBA saw an average 3.4× salary increase within 2 years.
Can a Staff Nurse become a Hospital CEO?
Yes — and it has already happened multiple times in our alumni network. Arjun Nair (featured above) went from Staff Nurse to COO in 4 years. Vijaya Lakshmi founded her own diagnostics company. Clinical experience is actually your biggest competitive advantage over pure MBA graduates when it comes to hospital leadership.
Can MBBS Doctors do this MBA? What's the benefit?
Yes, and doctors typically see the fastest salary jumps because they already carry institutional credibility. An MBBS doctor with healthcare management skills is the ideal candidate for Medical Director, VP Clinical Operations, and Healthcare Consulting roles. Dr. Ramesh Iyer in our alumni stories went from ₹9 LPA to ₹38 LPA.
What is the expected salary after MBA in Healthcare Management?
Entry-level post-MBA roles typically start at ₹8–12 LPA. With 2–3 years of experience in a management role, ₹15–25 LPA is common. Senior roles (COO, CEO, Medical Director) range from ₹25–60 LPA. Our average alumni salary at 5 years post-MBA is ₹21.4 LPA.
Is an online MBA in healthcare management accepted by hospitals?
Yes — in fact 94% of our placements come from hospitals who know our program specifically. What matters to hospital HR heads is the institute's reputation and the practical skills you demonstrate, not whether it was classroom or online. Our industry connections ensure our graduates are recognized immediately.
How long is the MBA program?
The full MBA program is 2 years (4 semesters) delivered as weekend online live sessions. For working professionals, we also offer an Executive PG Diploma (1 year) with a fast-track pathway to the full MBA degree. You can start either program while continuing your current job.
What are the placement opportunities? How does placement support work?
Our placement cell includes: resume and LinkedIn optimization, mock interview preparation, access to our exclusive job portal with 200+ hospital partners, referral introductions to senior alumni in hiring positions, and career counselling sessions every semester. We take placement seriously — our success is measured by your salary, not your exam scores.
What is the fee structure? Are EMIs available?
Fees are affordable and structured for working professionals. EMIs are available starting from ₹4,999/month with zero cost EMI partnerships. Exact fee details are shared during your free counselling call, as we also offer need-based scholarships and early-bird discounts. Book your call to get a personalised fee plan.
Is GNM Nurse eligible for this MBA?
Yes. GNM nurses with 1+ year of work experience are eligible. Many of our most successful alumni are GNM graduates who wanted a career upgrade. Your clinical experience counts as much as your academic background here.
Can I do this MBA while working full-time in a hospital?
Yes — this program is specifically designed for working healthcare professionals. All live sessions are on weekends (Saturday and Sunday) from 9 AM to 1 PM. Recorded sessions are available for any class you miss. The workload is designed to be manageable alongside 8-hour hospital shifts.
